National Convention Trips:

In high school, I was in a lot of clubs. A few of which I qualified for national competitions in. In BPA (Business Professionals of America) I made it to Nationals 2 yaers in a row and they were located in Chicago, IL (2024); and Orlando, FL (2025). They were both very fun places to go, but I think that Chicago was my favorite. The event that I qualified in was called Desktop Publishing, where I was given three prompts to create in under 90 minutes. In Chicago I placed 16th in the Nation.

Close-Up:

During my Junior and Senior year we fundraised for a trip to New York and Washington DC with a program called Close-Up. It was a really good experience to see all of the history and the monuments there.

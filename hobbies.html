Snowboarding and Snowmobiling:

I love winter sports, they are some of my favorite things to do in the winter. I have been around skiing/snowboarding and snowmobiling my whole life. I skiied for 9 years, starting at age 3; and then I started snowboarding when I was 12 and have been on a board for 6 years now. I like it a lot more and I think it is way more fun! I started riding snowmobiles with my family when I was really young. We take trips down to West Yellowstone and Island Park every once in a while to ride for the weekend. It's really cool down there and there's always so much snow!
